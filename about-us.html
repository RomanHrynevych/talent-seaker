about us

mission
statement.

We’re on a mission to deliver a professional and positive recruitment experience to everyone we work with, creating win-win outcomes for our clients and candidates.

talent seeker is a specialist headhunting business.

We source, engage and hire the very best talent for companies working in the Marketing, Digital and Property sectors throughout the UK.

We are particularly passionate about support tech start-ups with fast scale-up plans and charities.

What makes Talent Seeker so unique, is our ability to combine the advanced sourcing skills and techniques of a high-end Executive Search firm, with the engagement, service delivery and personalised approach of an Inhouse Recruitment Team.

We’re experts in Executive Search and Headhunting using social media. By engaging with Talent Seeker, you will reach the best passive talent in your sector – people who are open to looking at new opportunities, but aren’t active on online job boards. This means your company and opportunity can get in front of 100% of the population and not just the 20% who are active job seekers.

We are committed to working with clients and candidates for the long-term. We are an agile recruitment agency with clients ranging from start-ups, to multi-million pound global businesses with 1000s of employees.

Based in Fordingbridge, Hampshire, we support local companies in Ringwood, Southampton, Bournemouth, Poole, Salisbury and Winchester. We also have clients further afield in places like Chippenham, Gloucester, Brighton, London and Birmingham.

We focus on delivering the most positive outcomes for candidates and clients and it is this person centred approach that makes us a trusted recruitment partner of choice in our specialist sectors.

charity partner –
julia’s house.

We are proud to have partnered with the amazing local charity, Julia’s House since 2017.

Talent Seeker provide Julia’s House with a monthly donation to help support the incredible work they do in providing practical and emotional support for families caring for a child with a life-limiting or life-threatening condition, providing frequent and regular support in their own homes, in the community or at the Julia’s House hospices.
